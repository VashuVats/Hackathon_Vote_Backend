About

An online voting system that will replace the old ballot system or paper system. Over time, we have utilized technology in every sector to improve efficiency and save resources. But the voting system remains expensive and requires a large workforce. The system is slower and still not completely tamper-proof. We present a system that is safe, reliable, and addresses modern issues like broader accessibility, crowd-free voting, cost efficiency, faster results, and more.
